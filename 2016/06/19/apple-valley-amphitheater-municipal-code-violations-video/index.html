Apple Valley Amphitheater municipal code violations (video)

Posted on June 19, 2016 by Greg Raven

By Kerry Henard

So, as you can see in these videos and pictures, the Town of Apple Valley would fail its own code enforcement P.M.I. inspection.

If the Town’s Building Department were to perform an electrical inspection, the Town would fail.

Three different videos show three different attempts by the Town to bring the Amphitheater electrical up to national electrical code standards and the Town’s own municipal code standards. And they failed!

If the town were to own our municipal water system, the Town would fail!

These videos are made possible by the Town Council and Frank Robinson, Town Manager.
