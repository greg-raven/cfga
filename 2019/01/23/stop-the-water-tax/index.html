Stop the water tax

Posted on January 23, 2019 by Greg Raven

The Town Council has insisted for years that water costs are too high in Apple Valley, and that a majority of the citizens agree.

If that’s so, then I expect everyone in Apple Valley will want to sign the petition to stop Gavin Newsom’s proposed water tax, which can be found online at stopthewatertax.org.

Of course, this effort isn’t limited to citizens in Apple Valley. Anyone can sign the petition and help fight this new tax proposal.

Greg Raven, Apple Valley, CA
