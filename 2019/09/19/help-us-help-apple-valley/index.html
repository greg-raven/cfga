Help us help Apple Valley

Posted on September 19, 2019 by Greg Raven

Citizens for Government Accountability is issuing a call for any and all current and/or past residents of Apple Valley to help convince the San Bernardino County Grand Jury to look into what we believe are the many cases of misfeasance and malfeasance by employees and elected officials of the Town of Apple Valley.

To participate, all you need to do is to write up your observations and concerns about the governance and operation of the Town of Apple Valley and submit them to the Grand Jury. Be certain to include a section in which you offer a suggestion about how you would like the Grand Jury to act on your complaint.

The Grand Jury website has a page for submitting complaints. If you experience any difficulty submitting your complaint through the website, you can also submit your complaint to the Grand Jury assistant:

Norma Grosjean
Superior Court of San Bernardino County, Grand Jury
172 West Third Street, Second Floor
San Bernardino CA 92415-0243
E-mail: norma.grosjean@gj.sbcounty.gov

If the Grand Jury decides to investigate your complaint, you may be called for an interview, at which time you should be able to provide supporting documents and information.

Our goal is to get concerned citizens to submit enough substantive complaints that the Grand Jury understands how grave the situation is and takes action.
