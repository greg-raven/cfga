Letter: Good news, bad news (Schepis)

Posted on August 27, 2018 by Greg Raven

The good news is Doug Robertson reduced Apple Valley employee compensation by eliminating high level jobs in the amount of $1,139,151 (annually).

The bad news is the Town Council managed to squander $484,505 using Town money to improve the property value for two council members by paving and adding sidewalks to their privately owned property (John Glenn Road).

One hand is working hard to get us out of trouble, the other is scratching its own bottom.

This may not be criminal, but it sure should be …

Elections are coming — in with the new, out with the old.

Dean Schepis, Apple Valley

Source: Daily Press
