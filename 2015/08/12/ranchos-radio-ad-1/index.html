Ranchos radio ad #1 (audio)

Posted on August 12, 2015 by Greg Raven

Ranchos has two great new radio ads. Here’s the first one:


TOAV: We need to buy the Apple Valley Ranchos’ water system because government does it better and cheaper.

Consumer: How many water systems have you run?

TOAV: Well, we had one with nine connections, but we sold it.

Consumer: To who?

TOAV: Apple Valley Ranchos Water Company.

Consumer: What was the reason?

TOAV: Well, um …

Consumer: No thanks. Count us out.

Announcer: It’s crystal clear. The government takeover of Ranchos will only cost our community. Get the facts at AppleValleyWaterFacts.com
