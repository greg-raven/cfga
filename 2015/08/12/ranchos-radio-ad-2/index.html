Ranchos Radio Ad #2 (audio)

Posted on August 12, 2015 by Greg Raven

Ranchos has two great new radio ads. Here’s the second one:



TOAV: We need to buy the Apple Valley Ranchos’ water system because government does it better and cheaper.

Consumer: And you’ll lower rates?

TOAV: Well, no, I won’t do that.

Consumer: But you won’t raise rates?

TOAV: Actually, they have to go up.

Consumer: What about my taxes?

TOAV: Those will probably go up, too.

Consumer: Higher taxes and higher rates? Count us out.

Announcer: It’s crystal clear. The government takeover of Ranchos will only cost our community. Get the facts at AppleValleyWaterFacts.com
